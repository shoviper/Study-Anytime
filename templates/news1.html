    Software Engineer Skill Mapping Workshop



    Hello, Students of Software Engineering Program

    Since, the program provide several specialization option in the second half (Year 3 and 4) of the program. We realize that providing guildelines for the specialization is crucial for 2nd students to choose their own specialize. So KMITL and software engineering program presenting, "Software Engineer Skill Mapping Workshop"...


    Timing :

    The workshop will be held on every Friday from 10th Febuary to 3rd March at 804 ECC building from 9 AM to 12 PM.


    Topic :

    The workshop topic will be:

    • In Friday 10th Febuary and 17th Febuary - Topic about Metavearse : HTML5 and A-Frame technology.
    • In Friday 24th Febuary and 3rd March - Topic about Industrial IoT : IoT and Scada technology.

    The workshop will be open for second year student of Software Engineering Program and any students interrest in the topic.
